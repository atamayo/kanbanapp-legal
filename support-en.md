# KanbanApp Support

Need help with KanbanApp? Use this page for support, troubleshooting, and contact.

## Contact

For support requests, questions, or bug reports, please open an issue:

https://github.com/atamayo/kanbanapp-legal/issues

Please include your device model, iOS version, app version, and a short description of the problem.

## Common Questions

### Where is my task data stored?

KanbanApp stores your tasks on your device. If iCloud sync is enabled, Apple may sync your app data across your signed-in devices.

### How do I delete my data?

You can delete tasks directly in the app. You can also remove the app from your device to delete local app data.

### Why does the app ask for camera, photo, microphone, or speech permissions?

These permissions are used only when you choose quick capture features such as photo, scan, voice, or dictation input.

### How do I report a bug?

Open a support issue at the link above and describe what happened, what you expected, and the steps to reproduce the issue.
