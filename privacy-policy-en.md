# Privacy Policy for KanbanApp

Effective date: May 16, 2026

KanbanApp is a personal kanban and task management app designed to help you organize work, limit work in progress, and track progress.

This Privacy Policy explains what information KanbanApp handles and how it is used.

## Information You Create in the App

KanbanApp lets you create and manage tasks, including task titles, descriptions, priorities, status, completion criteria, and related task metadata such as creation and update dates.

This information is used only to provide the app's task management, dashboard, search, quick capture, progress, and focus features.

## Local Storage

KanbanApp stores your task data on your device using Apple's local persistence technologies.

The developer does not operate a separate server for storing your task content and does not sell your task data.

## iCloud Sync

If iCloud syncing is enabled and available, KanbanApp may sync your task data through Apple's iCloud/CloudKit services so your data can be available across your devices.

iCloud data is handled by Apple under your Apple ID and Apple's privacy terms. The developer does not directly access your iCloud account.

## Camera, Photos, Microphone, and Speech Recognition

KanbanApp may request access to certain device features to support quick capture:

- Camera access may be used to scan text and turn it into a task draft.
- Photo library access may be used to import an image and extract text for a task draft.
- Microphone access may be used to record dictated task ideas.
- Speech recognition may be used to transcribe dictated task ideas into text.

These permissions are optional. If you do not grant them, the related quick capture features may not work, but you can still create tasks manually.

## Notifications and App Badge

KanbanApp may use local notifications and app badge counts to remind you about aging, stalled, or active tasks.

These reminders are generated for app functionality and are not used for advertising.

## Data Sharing

KanbanApp does not sell your personal information.

KanbanApp does not share your task content with third-party advertisers or data brokers.

Your data may be processed by Apple services when you use system features such as iCloud, CloudKit, camera, photos, microphone, speech recognition, notifications, or other iOS platform services.

## Analytics and Tracking

KanbanApp does not use third-party advertising tracking.

KanbanApp does not track you across apps and websites owned by other companies.

## Children's Privacy

KanbanApp is not designed to knowingly collect personal information from children. If you believe a child has provided personal information through the app, please contact the developer so the issue can be reviewed.

## Data Deletion

You can delete tasks inside the app where deletion or archiving features are available.

You can also remove app data by deleting the app from your device. If iCloud sync is enabled, you may also need to manage or delete related iCloud data through your Apple ID or iCloud settings.

## Changes to This Policy

This Privacy Policy may be updated from time to time. Updates will be posted on this page with a new effective date.
